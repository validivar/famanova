✦ Our Story

About Famanova

What is Famanova?

Famanova is a free, AI-powered daily intelligence brief — designed for busy professionals, entrepreneurs, students, and curious minds who want to stay informed without spending an hour scrolling through news apps.

Every morning, Famanova automatically curates the top 5 global stories, pulls live financial market data, reads your local weather, and delivers a daily insight — all in one clean, fast-loading page. No account. No download. No clutter.

Our Mission

Information should be accessible to everyone. We believe the world's most important news, markets, and insights should take less than 60 seconds to absorb — and should be available to anyone, anywhere, for free.

From New York to London, São Paulo to Sydney, Dubai to Singapore, Cape Town to Nairobi — Famanova serves the same high-quality brief, tailored to your local weather and global moment.

How It Works

  • News: Live global headlines sourced from multiple news APIs and summarised for clarity. Updated on every page load.
  • Markets: Real-time prices for S&P 500, NASDAQ, Bitcoin, Ethereum, Gold and Oil — refreshed every 60 seconds.
  • Weather: Auto-detects your city via GPS and fetches live conditions from OpenWeatherMap.
  • Insights: Daily wisdom quotes and AI-curated facts to spark thinking.

How We Stay Free

Famanova is supported by affiliate partnerships (NordVPN, NordPass, Hostinger, Morning Brew) and newsletter sponsorships. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we earn a small commission — at no cost to you. This is what keeps the lights on and the page free forever.
